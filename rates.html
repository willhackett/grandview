Extras

Golf buggy: 

For guests staying at the house without a 4WD, a 4 person buggy is available on request.

Very strict conditions apply. Refundable Buggy security bond ($500). Walking access to the resort is a short 10 minute downhill walk.

If you have a 4WD, the buggy is not necessary for resort access because buggy parking and 4WD parking are together at the southern end of the resort.

 

Bed linen:

Linen is available for rent to interstate and overseas visitors. ($15/single bed and $25 queen/double bed).

This is because we have no access to a commercial laundry on the island.
